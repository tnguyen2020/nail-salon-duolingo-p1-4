Nail Salon Conversation - Lesson Plan

📋 Lesson Overview

Topic

Greeting a walk-in client and offering pedicure services

Level

Beginner (A1-A2)

Target Students

Vietnamese nail technicians learning English

Duration

15-20 minutes

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Greet clients and ask about appointments
  • Accept walk-in customers politely
  • Ask which service the client wants
  • Describe pedicure options with time durations
  • Direct clients to a specific chair/seat

📖 Key Vocabulary

Word / Phrase Pronunciation Vietnamese Hint Example
appointment /əˈpɔɪntmənt/ lịch hẹn "Do you have an appointment?"
walk in /wɑːk ɪn/ đến không cần hẹn "Can I walk in?"
service /ˈsɜːrvɪs/ dịch vụ "Which service would you like?"
basic /ˈbeɪsɪk/ cơ bản "A basic pedicure for 30 minutes."
deluxe /dɪˈlʌks/ cao cấp "A deluxe pedicure for 50 minutes."
pedicure /ˈpɛdɪkjʊr/ làm móng chân "I would like the spa pedicure."
of course /əv kɔːrs/ tất nhiên "Yes, of course!"
perfect /ˈpɜːrfɪkt/ tuyệt vời "Perfect! Please go to chair number 2."

💬 Full Dialog

Nail Tech: "Hello! Do you have an appointment?"
Client: "No, I don't. Can I walk in?"
Nail Tech: "Yes, of course! Which service would you like?"
Client: "What do you have?"
Nail Tech: "We have a basic pedicure for 30 minutes, a spa pedicure for 40 minutes, and a deluxe pedicure for 50 minutes."
Client: "I would like the spa pedicure, please."
Nail Tech: "Perfect! Please go to chair number 2."

🎮 Game Structure (6 Rounds)

Round 1: Fill in the Blank

Line: "Hello! Do you have an ___?"

Answer: appointment

Skill: Greeting + key vocabulary

Round 2: Choose the Response

Client says: "No, I don't. Can I walk in?"

Correct: "Yes, of course! Which service would you like?"

Skill: Polite acceptance + follow-up question

Round 3: Unscramble

Words: do / have / What / you / ?

Answer: "What do you have?"

Skill: Question formation

Round 4: Match Services

Match: Service type → Time duration

Skill: Vocabulary for describing services

Round 5: Fill the Order

Line: "I would like the ___ pedicure, please."

Answer: spa

Skill: Listening to client preference

Round 6: Build the Sentence

Words: Perfect / Please / go / to / chair / number / 2

Answer: "Perfect! Please go to chair number 2."

Skill: Directing the client

💡 Teaching Tips

🗣️

Practice Out Loud

Have students read the Nail Tech lines out loud before playing. Focus on clear pronunciation of "appointment," "service," and "deluxe."

🔄

Role Play After

After the game, pair students up. One is the Nail Tech, one is the Client. Switch roles after one round.

✍️

Extend the Dialog

Ask students: "What else could you say?" Add lines like "Would you like a drink?" or "Your total is \$45."

📱

Real Salon Practice

Encourage students to use these exact phrases at work tomorrow. Start with just the greeting: "Hello! Do you have an appointment?"
