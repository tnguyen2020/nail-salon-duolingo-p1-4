🆕 Scene 1: New Client Arrives

You're a first-time customer. See if the student can greet you properly!

1
👩 YOU SAY (Customer):
"Hi! I'm new here."
Xin chào! Tôi mới đến đây.
💡 Wait for the student to greet you and introduce themselves.
2
💁‍♀️ STUDENT SHOULD SAY:
"Hello! My name is ___. Nice to meet you!"
✅ Check: Did they say their name? Did they say "nice to meet you"?
3
👩 YOU SAY:
"Nice to meet you too! My name is Sarah."
Tôi cũng rất vui! Tên tôi là Sarah.
4
💁‍♀️ STUDENT SHOULD ASK:
"Is this your first time here?" or "Do you have an appointment?"
✅ Check: Did they ask a relevant question?
5
👩 YOU SAY:
"Yes, it's my first time! I don't have an appointment. Is that okay?"
Vâng, lần đầu tiên! Tôi không có lịch hẹn. Được không?
6
💁‍♀️ STUDENT SHOULD RESPOND:
Welcome them! "Of course! Welcome!" / "No problem!"

✅ Assessment Checklist

Said "Hello" or greeted the customer
Introduced themselves ("My name is...")
Said "Nice to meet you"
Asked "Is this your first time?"
Asked about appointment
Welcomed the customer warmly
0 / 6 ✅

🔄 Scene 2: Returning Client

You're a regular customer. See if the student remembers "Welcome back!"

1
👩 YOU SAY:
"Hi! I'm back again!"
Xin chào! Tôi trở lại rồi!
💡 See if the student says "Welcome back!"
2
💁‍♀️ STUDENT SHOULD SAY:
"Welcome back!" / "Good to see you again!"
3
👩 YOU SAY:
"Yes, I have an appointment at 2 o'clock. I also brought my sister today!"
Vâng, tôi có lịch hẹn lúc 2 giờ. Hôm nay tôi cũng mang theo chị gái!
4
💁‍♀️ STUDENT SHOULD:
Welcome the sister too! "Nice to meet you!" / "Welcome!"

✅ Assessment Checklist

Said "Welcome back!"
Asked about appointment
Acknowledged the sister
Said "Nice to meet you" to sister
0 / 4 ✅

👨‍👩‍👧 Scene 3: Family Small Talk

During the appointment, see if the student can make family small talk!

1
💁‍♀️ STUDENT SHOULD ASK:
A family question: "Do you have children?" / "Are you married?"
💡 If the student doesn't ask, you can start by mentioning your family.
2
👩 YOU SAY:
"Yes! I have 3 kids. This is my husband waiting outside."
Vâng! Tôi có 3 người con. Đây là chồng tôi đang đợi bên ngoài.
3
💁‍♀️ STUDENT SHOULD RESPOND:
"Oh nice!" + follow-up question: "Are they going to school?"
4
👩 YOU SAY:
"Yes, one is going to primary school and the other two go to college."
Vâng, một đứa đang học tiểu học và hai đứa kia học đại học.
5
👩 YOU ASK BACK:
"What about you? Do you have kids?"
Còn bạn thì sao? Bạn có con không?
💡 The student should answer about their own family!
6
💁‍♀️ STUDENT SHOULD:
Answer about their family: "Yes, I have..." or "No, I don't have..."

✅ Assessment Checklist

Asked "Do you have children/kids?"
Responded to family info naturally
Asked follow-up question (school, age, etc.)
Answered about their own family
Used family vocabulary correctly
0 / 5 ✅
